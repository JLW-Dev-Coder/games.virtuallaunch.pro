# MARKET — games.virtuallaunch.pro (GVLP)

Last updated: 2026-04-04

---

## 1. Product Definition

**GVLP** is a gamified engagement platform for tax professionals. Operators (tax pros) embed interactive mini-games on their client-facing websites to boost engagement, educate visitors on tax topics, and drive repeat traffic.

- **Core promise**: Turn tax education into interactive fun that keeps clients coming back
- **Revenue model**: Tiered monthly subscriptions ($0 / $9 / $19 / $39)

## 2. Target Audience (ICP)

**Primary**: Independent tax preparers and small tax firms who want to differentiate their online presence and increase client engagement.

**Secondary**: Accounting firms, enrolled agents, and tax educators looking for interactive tools to supplement their content.

## 3. Problems Solved

| Pain Point | Value Delivered |
|---|---|
| Low client engagement on tax websites | Interactive games drive repeat visits |
| Tax education is dry and forgettable | Gamification makes learning sticky |
| Hard to differentiate from other tax pros | Unique embed games are a competitive edge |
| No easy way to monetize website traffic | Token-based play creates engagement metrics |

## 4. Pricing

| Tier | Monthly | Tokens | Games | Target Segment |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | Free | 100 | 1 | Try-before-you-buy |
| Apprentice | $9 | 500 | 3 | Solo preparers |
| Strategist | $19 | 1,500 | 6 | Growing practices |
| Navigator | $39 | 5,000 | 9 (all) | Firms / power users |

## 5. Competitive Landscape

No direct competitor offers tax-specific gamified embeds. Indirect alternatives:
- Generic quiz builders (Typeform, Interact) — not tax-specific, no token economy
- Tax education platforms (IRS.gov, CPE providers) — not embeddable, not gamified
- Client portals (Canopy, TaxDome) — different use case, no engagement games

**Differentiator**: Purpose-built for tax pros, drop-in embed, token-gated play.

## 6. Go-to-Market

- **Primary channel**: SCALE outreach (email sequences to tax professionals)
- **Secondary**: SEO via asset pages on virtuallaunch.pro, organic referrals
- **Messaging**: "Gamify your tax practice. Embed interactive games your clients will love."

## 7. Tone and Voice

- Professional but energetic — tax pros take their work seriously, but games should feel fun
- No jargon-heavy marketing — clear, benefit-driven language
- Confidence without hype — "boost engagement" not "revolutionize your practice"
